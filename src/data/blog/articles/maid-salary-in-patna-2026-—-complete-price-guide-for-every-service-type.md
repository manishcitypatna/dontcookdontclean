## Why Maid Salary in Patna Varies So Much
 
Before looking at numbers, it helps to understand why the range is so wide. Several factors directly affect what you will pay:
 
**Type of work** — Cleaning-only work costs less than cooking. Cooking plus cleaning costs more than either alone.
 
**Hours per day** — A 1-hour visit costs far less than an 8-hour full-time shift, obviously, but the per-hour rate also drops as hours increase.
 
**Number of meals or rooms** — A 2-BHK with one meal a day costs less than a 4-BHK with three meals daily.
 
**Area within Patna** — Boring Road, Kankarbagh, and Patliputra Colony tend to run slightly higher than outer or newly developing areas, though the gap is usually small.
 
**Experience and skill** — A cook who can prepare multiple cuisines or handle specific dietary needs (diabetic meals, Jain food, baby food) typically charges more than a basic helper.
 
**Caste and local hiring practices** — This is a reality discussed often in local Patna communities, including Reddit threads. While we do not encourage this as a basis for hiring decisions, it does influence informal pricing in unregulated, word-of-mouth hiring.

## What About the "₹49 Per Hour" or "₹599 Per Day" Advertised Rates?
 
You may have come across listings advertising maid services at ₹49 per hour or ₹599 per day. These numbers are technically accurate but often misleading for anyone planning a regular monthly arrangement.
 
Here is what usually happens with these rates:
 
- They typically apply to **one-time or trial bookings only**, not ongoing monthly help
- There is often a **minimum visit charge** that makes the effective hourly cost higher than advertised
- Some platforms charge a **separate booking or convenience fee** on top of the hourly rate
- These rates rarely include verification, background checks, or replacement support
If you do the math on a real monthly basis — say 1 hour daily for 26-30 days — the ₹49/hour rate would suggest roughly ₹1,300 to ₹1,500 per month, which is actually in line with the lower end of real market rates for basic cleaning. So the number itself is not fake, but it is usually advertised to attract attention rather than reflect a comprehensive, verified, ongoing service.

## Why Prices Differ Between Agencies and Informal Hiring
 
**Informal hiring (neighbour referral, local contact)** tends to be cheaper because there is no verification, no agency margin, and pricing is purely negotiated between you and the helper directly.
 
**Agency-based hiring** costs slightly more because it typically includes:
- ID verification and background checks
- Replacement support if the arrangement does not work out
- A structured process for resolving issues
- Accountability if something goes wrong
The price difference is usually modest — often just ₹300 to ₹1,000 more per month — but the peace of mind and safety it provides is significant, especially for households with children, elderly members, or when both spouses are away at work during the day.
