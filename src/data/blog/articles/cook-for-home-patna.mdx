## Types of Home Cooks Available in Patna

Understanding what type of cook you need saves time during the search and avoids expensive mismatches.

### Part-Time Cook
Comes for **1 to 2 hours daily**, usually in the morning or evening. Prepares one or two meals — typically breakfast and lunch, or just dinner. This is the most commonly hired type of home cook in Patna.

Best for: Working couples, small families, or anyone who just needs help with one meal a day.

### Half-Day Cook
Works **4 to 5 hours** and typically handles breakfast, lunch, and kitchen tidying. More thorough than a part-time arrangement.

Best for: Families with school-going children who need morning and afternoon meal support.

### Full-Time Day Cook
Works **8 to 10 hours daily**, managing all three meals plus kitchen organisation and sometimes coordination with household grocery needs.

Best for: Larger families, joint family setups, or homes where cooking is a significant daily responsibility.

### Live-In Cook
Lives at the home and manages all cooking needs around the clock. Usually comes as part of a broader household management arrangement.

Best for: Elderly households, families requiring round-the-clock meal support, or households where other domestic help is also live-in.

## What a Home Cook Typically Does — And What They Don't

One of the most common sources of conflict when hiring a cook for home is unclear expectations from the start. Here is a realistic breakdown.

**A home cook typically handles:**
- Preparing meals as per family preference and dietary needs
- Basic kitchen hygiene while cooking
- Washing utensils used during cooking (if agreed upon)
- Informing when kitchen supplies are running low

**A home cook typically does not handle:**
- Full house cleaning or sweeping and mopping
- Laundry or clothes washing
- Childcare or babysitting
- Grocery shopping (unless specifically agreed and compensated)

If you need cooking plus cleaning, you are looking for a **cook-cum-maid** arrangement, which is a different role with different expectations and salary. Be clear about this before hiring.

## 5 Things to Check Before Hiring a Home Cook in Patna

### 1. ID and Address Verification
Ask for Aadhaar card or any government-issued photo ID. Confirm the address matches someone local to Patna. Keep a photocopy on file.

### 2. Cooking Trial
This is non-negotiable. Ask the cook to prepare a meal your family eats regularly. You will immediately know if the taste, hygiene, and method match your expectations. No amount of conversation replaces a cooking trial.

### 3. Previous Work References
Ask for at least one reference from a family they have worked with in Patna. Call and ask directly about punctuality, hygiene habits, and reliability.

### 4. Health and Hygiene Awareness
A home cook should understand basic food hygiene — washing hands before cooking, keeping raw and cooked food separate, not cooking when unwell. These are simple things but important to observe during the trial.

### 5. Communication Style
Can they understand your cooking preferences clearly? Do they ask questions or just assume? A cook who communicates well adjusts faster and causes fewer problems in the long run.

## Common Mistakes Families Make When Hiring a Home Cook

**Not doing a cooking trial** — The biggest mistake. Always trial before committing.

**Unclear salary agreement** — Verbal agreements lead to disputes. Write it down, even informally.

**Not setting a notice period** — If the cook wants to leave or you want to let them go, both sides need advance notice. Agree on 15 to 30 days upfront.

**Skipping reference checks** — Takes 5 minutes and can save months of trouble.

**Hiring through unverified sources** — Classified sites and random contacts carry real risk. An unverified person in your home daily is a safety concern, not just a cooking concern.
