# A Weekly House Cleaning Checklist for Busy Families

Keeping your home clean doesn't have to consume your weekends. Use this weekly checklist to stay on top of tasks efficiently.

## Monday: Kitchen Deep Clean

- Wipe all countertops and appliances
- Clean the microwave inside and out
- Sanitize the sink
- Mop the floor
- Take out the trash and recycling

## Tuesday: Bathrooms

- Scrub toilets, sinks, and showers
- Clean mirrors and fixtures
- Mop floors
- Replace towels
- Restock supplies

## Wednesday: Dusting

- Dust all surfaces
- Clean ceiling fans and light fixtures
- Wipe baseboards
- Dust electronics (with care!)
- Clean window sills

## Thursday: Vacuuming

- Vacuum all carpets and rugs
- Vacuum upholstery
- Vacuum under furniture
- Empty vacuum bag or canister

## Friday: Tidy and Organize

- Put away clutter
- Organize entryway
- Straighten up living spaces
- Prepare for the weekend

## Weekend: Light Maintenance

- Do any remaining tasks
- Plan for the coming week
- Deep clean one area (optional)

By breaking down cleaning into manageable daily tasks, you'll maintain a clean home without feeling overwhelmed!
