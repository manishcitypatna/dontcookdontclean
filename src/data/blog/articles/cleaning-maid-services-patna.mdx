## What Cleaning Maid Services in Patna Actually Cover

Before hiring, it helps to understand what a cleaning maid typically does — and what falls outside their scope. Mismatched expectations are the number one reason household arrangements break down within the first few weeks.

### Standard Cleaning Tasks
A household cleaning maid in Patna typically handles:

- **Sweeping and mopping** — all rooms, daily or as agreed
- **Dusting** — furniture, shelves, fans, and surfaces
- **Bathroom cleaning** — toilets, floors, sinks, and fixtures
- **Utensil washing** — kitchen vessels and dishes after meals
- **Kitchen surface cleaning** — countertops, stovetop, and sink
- **Garbage collection** — collecting and taking out household waste
- **General tidying** — keeping rooms organised and clutter-free

### Tasks That Are Separate or Additional
- Cooking or meal preparation
- Laundry and clothes washing (often a separate arrangement)
- Deep cleaning or annual cleaning (typically charged separately)
- Childcare or babysitting
- Grocery shopping

If you need a combination of cleaning and cooking, ask specifically for a **cook-cum-maid** arrangement. This is a distinct role and should be agreed upon clearly before hiring.

## Types of Cleaning Maid Services Available in Patna

### Part-Time Cleaning Maid
The most common arrangement. The maid comes for **1 to 2 hours daily**, handles the core cleaning tasks, and leaves. Works best for smaller homes or families who only need daily maintenance cleaning.

### Half-Day Maid
Works **4 to 5 hours** and covers more thorough cleaning — all rooms, bathrooms, kitchen, and sometimes laundry. Suitable for medium-sized homes with heavier cleaning needs.

### Full-Time Maid
Works **8 to 10 hours daily** and manages all household cleaning comprehensively, sometimes including cooking and laundry as part of the package. Best for larger homes or joint families.

### Live-In Maid
Lives at the home and provides round-the-clock household support including cleaning, cooking, and general domestic management. Ideal for elderly households or families needing continuous help.

## 6 Things to Check Before Hiring a Cleaning Maid in Patna

### 1. Government ID Verification
Ask for Aadhaar card or any government-issued photo ID. Note the details and keep a photocopy. This is a basic safety step that many families skip — and regret later.

### 2. Local Address Confirmation
Ensure the maid lives in or near Patna. Someone travelling from a distant area is more likely to be inconsistent with attendance over time, especially during festivals, rains, or personal situations.

### 3. Reference from Previous Employer
Call at least one previous family the maid has worked with. Ask specifically about attendance consistency, honesty, and quality of cleaning. Five minutes on the phone is worth months of peace of mind.

### 4. One-Week Trial Period
Start with a trial week before committing to a monthly arrangement. Observe punctuality, cleaning thoroughness, and whether they follow instructions properly.

### 5. Clear Task Agreement
Write down — even informally — exactly which tasks are included, working hours, salary, weekly off day, and notice period. Verbal agreements lead to disputes.

### 6. Background Check
If you are hiring through an agency, confirm that they conduct background checks as part of their placement process. A trusted maid placement service in Patna should do this as a standard step, not an optional extra.
